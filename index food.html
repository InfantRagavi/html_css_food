MASALA PASTA

Masala pasta is an indian-italian fusion dish that may have originated on the streets of india,
selling it as a street food.not a traditional dish but it has been a favourite and made in almost
every indian home,especially with kids.

HOW TO MAKE MASALA PASTA

PREPARATION

INGREDIENTS
  1. fine chop 1medium onion(½cup),2medium tomatoes(1¼cup chopped).
    mince 2to3 garlic cloves& ½ inch ginger to make 1½ to 2 teaspoons ginger garlic.


  2. peel and chop 1 medium carrot(½ cup) and ¼ cup bell peppers.
    ¼ cup green peas & 2 tablespoons young sweet corn.


  3. ¾ teaspoon salt,½ teaspoon garam masala,1 teaspoon coriander powder,
    ½ teaspoon kashmiri red chilli powder,1/8 teasppom turmeric,
    1 tablespoon kasuri methi(dried fenugreek leaves).
    • ½ teaspoon roasted cumin powder

    • 1 teaspoon tomato paste

    BOIL PASTA

  4. bring 6 cups water to a boil in a large pot.when the water begins to boil,
    add 1 teaspoon salt,1 ½ cups pasta & 1taplespoon oil.
  5. cook pasta on a medium flame.it will boil for 8 to 9 mins.
  6. drain the pasta to a colander.reserve ¾ cup pasta cooked water before draining.
    you can use this towards the end to bring the pasta to consistency.


    7. MAKE MASALA FOR PASTA

  7. heat 1½ tablespoons oil & 1½ tablespoon butter in a pan.add ½ teaspoon cumin seeds.
    when the seeds begin to sizzle,add garlic & ginger.fry for a minute.
  8. add the chopped onions and fry until they turn golden.
  9. add the chopped vegetable and fry for 2 to 3 minutes.
  10. stir in the salt & spices-red chilli powder,garam masala,coriander powder,turmeric,
    and also add cumin and fennel powder.
  11. add tomatoes and tomato ketchup.
  12. mix and cook on a medium to medium-high heat until the masala begins to smell good or release oil.
    make sure the smell of uncooked tomatoes is gone and your veggies are cooked through.
    if not reduce the heat and cook on a low heat until the veggies are tender.
  13. pour ¼ to ½ cup water or pasta cooked water or stock.mix well.
  14. cook until the masala becomes slightly thick,aromatic and saucy.do not dry out
    the masala completely at this stage.add the kasuri methi.


    15. MAKE MASALA PASTA

  15. add cooked pasta & mix well.if the masala is dry,splash more water.taste test and add more salt if needed.
    cover and cook on a low to medium heat until the pasta becomes hot,for about 2mins.
  16. sprinkle coriander leaves and serve masala pasta hot.add 2 to 3 tablespoon heavy cream or
    ¼ cup grated cheese on the hot pasta.
